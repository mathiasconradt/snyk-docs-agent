| Group        | Group Viewer     | group_viewer     |
| Group        | Group Member     | group_member     |
| Tenant       | Tenant Admin     | tenant_admin     |
| Tenant       | Tenant Viewer    | tenant_viewer    |
| Tenant       | Tenant Member    | tenant_member    |
