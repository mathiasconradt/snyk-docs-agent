Group Admins on a Snyk Enterprise plan who use SAML for SSO can configure Snyk Single Sign-on themselves. Ensure you have at least one Group and Organization where you can assign new users. See Manage Groups and Organizations.

To enable the self-serve SSO option, contact your Snyk account team or Snyk support.\
\
This option does not accommodate custom role mapping. To set up custom role mapping with SSO for your Snyk Group, contact your Snyk account team.
